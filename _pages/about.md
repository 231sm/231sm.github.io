<!-- ## About Me -->

Hi! I am Shumin Deng. 
I have been working as a ZJU100 Young Professor at <a href='https://ai.zju.edu.cn/'>Colledge of Artificial Intelligence, Zhejiang University</a> at present. 
Before then, I have worked as a Research Fellow in <a href='https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/cs/'>Department of Computer Science, School of Computing (SoC), National University of Singapore</a>, advised by <a href='https://bhooi.github.io/'>Bryan Hooi</a>. 
I have obtained my Ph.D. degree at <a href='http://www.cs.zju.edu.cn/'>School of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University</a>, with the supervision of <a href='http://mypage.zju.edu.cn/huajun'>Prof. Huajun Chen</a>. 
I have obtained my bachelor degree from <a href='https://cs.xidian.edu.cn/'>College of Software Engineering, Xidian University</a>.

<p><b>Research Interests: </b> <br> Natural Language Processing, LLM-Based Reasoning and Planning, LLM-Based Agents, Embodied AI, Human-AI Interaction, Model Steering, Structured Prediction, Information Extraction, Neuro-Symbolic Reasoning, Knowledge Graph. </p>
